Say Dil Se

Project information

  • Category: Mobile Application
  • Client: ADJAVIS DIGITAL LLP
  • Project URL: Say Dil Se
  • Video: Say Dil Se Ads

India's largest personalised e-card and e-greeting mobile app

Free Greeting Cards, Everyday Cards and Quotes for every occasions and reasons.
GREETINGS - Choose from birthday, anniversary and celebratory cards for festivals and special occasions. Browse by relationships like dad, mom, wife, husband, sister, brother, friend, grand parents or colleagues and skim through variety of moods like fun, romance, inspirational or serious.
EVERYDAY CARDS - Brighten up a friend or colleague's day with funny, optimistic, witty or inspirational good morning and good day messages. Let a loved one know how much you love them or miss them with sweet romantic love messages. Sincere apologise, heartfelt condolences, emphatic thank you cards are just a click away.
FUN - Wish away stress or boredom with hilarious, sarcastic, silly, cute and comical jokes and fun cards.
QUOTES - Inspire, motivate and encourage friends, colleagues, employees with insightful, smart, amusing, wise and philosophical quotes from the world's finest minds.

APP FEATURES:
- Personalised hand picked greeting with your name and the receiver's name
- Turn your pictures into beautiful shareable cards with custom filters, graphics and frames
- Quick, easy way to share greetings and quotes
- Hundred of cards added every week
- Special festive contests and updates
- Share on any messenger or social sharing app
