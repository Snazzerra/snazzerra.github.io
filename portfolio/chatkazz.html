Chatkazz - A Restaurant App

Project information

  • Category: Mobile Application
  • Client: A client of Australian Restaurant
  • Project URL: Chatkazz

A Australian Restaurant App

This is an official Mobile Ordering application of Chatkazz Australia.

It allows you to view and use the complete menu and content for the purpose of placing an order directly at Chatkazz Restaurants.

Food is our passion and at Chatkazz, we believe in creating the 'Indian experience with the best Indian Street Food and Farsan', which will re-kindle your memories of home. And even if you haven't been to India, come and join us to experience how delicious Indian street food is!

Choose your meal from the largest selection of restaurants, including Japanese restaurants, Thai and other restaurants. to ask your food Safari, up you wherever you are.

With over 200 dishes on our menu taking inspiration from the North and South of India, each visit to Chatkazz will bring back memories of home. Expect fragrant dishes packed with flavour, many which can rarely be found outside of India.
