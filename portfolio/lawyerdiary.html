Lawyer Dairy - Adovocate Digital Dairy

Project information

  • Category: React Native Application
  • Highlights: Registration, Notifications, Case Generation

LawyerDiaryApp (LDA) is an electronic assistant for modern Indian lawyers to improve the efficiency of the law practice and the law firm.

Few use cases that are very interesting to Lawyers are – LDA finds lawyers’ cases in the cause lists of high courts and Supreme Court and sends SMS and push notifications.

LDA will start monitoring the progress of cases in the respective court hall in the High courts and sends periodic updates about the progress of court hall. LDA will send Just-In-Time message to the lawyer, 5 cases before her/his case reaches in the court hall.

Lawyers never miss a case or waste their time by waiting in the court hall for case to reach. Most of the law firms don’t systematically track the to-dos, notes and appointments on their client cases, but the LDA enables them to start tracking.

Overall LDA enables the communication of statuses and progress of the case among the associates and clerks of the law firm.
