LTRconnect - A Long-Term Relationship Connections Start Here

Project information

  • Category: Flutter App
  • Client: Lindi Tardif - Author|Founder|Lawyer
  • Project Highlights: Flutter App with PostgreSQL, Go, REST api, Firebase Authentication
  • Android App: LTRconnect - Android
  • iOS App: LTRconnect - iOS

Get to heart of a long-term relationship

Let’s face it, everyone knows a profile peppered with a few photos isn’t going to help you figure out who your lifetime partner could be. There’s so much more you need to find out about a potential partner before you can figure that out. You need to know their background, values, likes, dislikes, fears, aspirations, and how those line up to yours so you can determine if you’re compatible. Swiping apps on the market right now aren’t geared at helping you with that. This is where LTRconnect is different.

LTRconnect peels back the curtain on what potential candidates are up to on a day-to-day basis, giving you greater visibility into their lives, so you can have that deeper level of engagement and connection with them before moving the relationship to the next level.
